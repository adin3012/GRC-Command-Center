Documentation

The Vantage Circle GRC Command Center is a centralized Governance, Risk and Compliance dashboard built to provide transparent and accessible oversight of Vantage Circle's security posture, compliance certifications, and policy framework.

This dashboard is intended for use by employees, clients, and external stakeholders. It offers a single organized view of all governance documentation and does not require a login to view or download any content.

What you can find here

Policy Library. All active, under review, and draft organizational policies covering information security, data privacy, human resources, and operations.

Compliance Reports. Audit reports including GDPR readiness assessments, SOC 2 evaluations, and internal compliance reviews, all available as downloadable PDFs.

VAPT Reports. Vulnerability Assessment and Penetration Testing reports for web applications, mobile platforms, and network infrastructure.

Certificates. Active compliance certifications held by Vantage Circle, including ISO 27001, ISO 27701, SOC 2 Type II, GDPR, HIPAA, CCPA, and DPDPA.

All documents are maintained and updated by the GRC administrator. Clients and stakeholders are welcome to download any report or certificate for due diligence, vendor assessments, or audit purposes.
